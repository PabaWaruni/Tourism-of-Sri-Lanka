our gallery

pearl of the indian ocean

scenic

The central highlands are carpeted with emerald tea plantations interspersed with occasional waterfalls. Cool climates, misty views, and quaint estate bungalows are a throwback to bygone eras.

essence

Sri Lankan food is like no other. Filled with aromatic spices and vibrant colour, Sri Lankan food leaves you feeling happy, warm, and content.

beach

The best of the Southern beaches include Tangalla, Beruwala, Mirissa, Bentota and Unawatuna with varying options including chic boutique hotels, glowing coral reefs, gentle sandbars and undiscovered corners of paradise.

wild

The country has long claimed to have the highest amphibian species density in the world with a high concentration in the Sinharaja rainforest.

heritage

With a history expanding over 3000years, Sri Lanka holds some of worlds ancient cities including Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa and Digamadulla

thrills

With varying climates and Geography packed into a small island Sri Lanka offers a range of adventures from the top of the mountains to the depths of the oceans.

festive

Every month brings a celebration either religious or cultural importance, making Sri Lanka one of the countries with highest number of celebrations and holidays.
